Project overview

The Product

This App was created for getting food. This App doesn’t only offer it’s standard functionality such as choosing a restaurant, adding your favourite dishes to the cart, and making an order. It gives free delivery and also lets you keep track of where your delivery driver is and get in touch with them if needed. Its also an app where people can scan there face for any food suggestion based on their mood.


Project Duration

The project was completed within a span of 8 weeks, demonstrating efficient time management and a focused approach to deliver results promptly.



Project Overview


User Research

Our user research for the Yummy Food Flash app involves understanding what people like to eat, how they order food, and what makes them happy while dining. Through surveys, interviews, and studying their social media behavior, we gather insights on their food preferences and cravings. This helps us create a user-friendly app with personalized food suggestions based on their mood, real-time delivery tracking, and free delivery. Our goal is to provide a satisfying and convenient food ordering experience tailored to their tastes
