Redesign of Aunua Global Website

About

I joined AUNUA Global as a volunteer UX UI Designer to contribute my skills and creativity to the organization and also in creating a user centered design, visual appealing and improving user experience .



AUNUA Global is a social impact organization that nurtures young creative minds to thrive and grow for a new age of kind leadership that our world needs right now. They strive toward building a better world for all children. Bridging communities together and engaging their hearts and minds is at the core of all we do, with the pillars of our ethos being Education, Economy, Environment and Empowerment.



I was approached by the CEO of Aunua Global to review the ongoing redesign of the Aunua Global platform. Following a productive Zoom meeting with the CEO, I gained valuable insights into the project's objectives. Through close collaboration with the technical and design teams, I successfully crafted a user-centric design, featuring an intuitive interface and meticulous implementation of user experience principles

.

Key Takeaways on Aunua Website Redesign

Clear Navigation: The website has a clear and intuitive navigation structure. Users should be able to easily find the information they need without confusion or frustration. Engaging Visual Design: We create a visually appealing design that aligns with Aunua Global's brand and mission. Utilize a harmonious color palette, meaningful imagery, and consistent typography to establish a cohesive visual identity. The design should evoke positive emotions and reflect the organization's values.

Prominent Call-to-Actions: We highlight essential actions and conversion points throughout the website by using clear and visually distinctive call-to-action (CTA) buttons and links that prompt users to take specific actions, such as signing up for newsletters, donating, or accessing educational resources.

Accessibility Considerations: The website meets accessibility guidelines, allowing individuals with disabilities to access and use the content effectively. Considering factors such as proper heading structure, alt text for images, sufficient color contrast, and keyboard navigation compatibility.

Emotional Well-being Focus: Reflect the importance of emotions and well-being through the design. Use imagery, colors, and typography that evoke positivity, comfort, and support. Create a welcoming atmosphere that resonates with the target audience children and their parents or caregivers.
